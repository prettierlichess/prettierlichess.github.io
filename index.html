Prettierlichess

It's lichess, but prettier.

Download it on the Chrome Web Store! Download it on the Firefox Add On Store! Check out the source code!
Lichess is a great platform that provides lots of fantastic free services like free analysis, studies, tournaments, and more. However, the site doesn't feel very contemporary, and this can turn many users off towards other platforms, and ultimately off an incredibly useful and amazing resource.

This extension is an attempt to fix that.

Prettier Lichess not only changes the visuals of lichess.org, but also allows users to completely customize the colors of the site as well. Via the extension they have control over the complete color palette. Along with this, they can import and export their color palette's at will. Below is a Google Drive iframe with some community color files. Click the button below it to add your own!
